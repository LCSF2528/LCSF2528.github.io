About Cheezstackz

CheezStackz is a modern dairy brand dedicated to producing high-quality, delicious, and affordable cheese slices for everyday meals. We focus on creating smooth, flavorful slices that melt perfectly and elevate any dish—whether it’s sandwiches, burgers, snacks, or home-cooked recipes.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of cheese enthusiasts, CheezStackz began with a simple mission: to make cheese slices that are both tasty and convenient. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted brand known for our commitment to quality ingredients, innovative flavors, and sustainable practices.

At CheezStackz, we believe that cheese is more than just a food item; it’s an experience that brings people together. Our team is passionate about crafting cheese slices that not only taste great but also inspire creativity in the kitchen. We are constantly exploring new flavors and techniques to ensure that our products meet the evolving tastes of our customers.

Times have changed, but Armstrong cheese has stood the test of time. For generations, our master cheesemakers have passed along their knowledge and expertise to bring you Cheddar cheese at its best. From our family to yours, we are proud to be a part of your meals and memories.

Why Choose Cheezstackz?

  1. Quality Ingredients: We source the finest milk and natural ingredients to ensure our cheese slices are rich in flavor and texture.
  2. Variety of Flavors: From classic Cheddar to adventurous blends like Jalapeño Jack and Smoked Gouda, we offer a wide range of flavors to suit every palate.
  3. Convenience: Our cheese slices are designed for easy use, whether you’re making a quick snack or preparing a gourmet meal.
  4. Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally friendly practices, from sourcing to packaging, to reduce our carbon footprint.
  5. Customer Satisfaction: We value our customers and strive to exceed their expectations with every product we deliver.

Our Guarantee

At CheezStackz, we stand behind the quality of our cheese slices. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team for assistance. Your satisfaction is our top priority!
